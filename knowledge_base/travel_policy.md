# Travel Policy

## Flight Booking
Domestic flights should be economy class.
International flights over 6 hours may use premium economy with manager approval.

## Hotel Limits
Recommended nightly cap is $180 for domestic travel and $250 for international travel.
Exceptions require pre-approval.

## Per Diem
Meal allowance is $45 per day for domestic travel and $65 for international travel.
Taxi and public transport are reimbursable with receipts.
