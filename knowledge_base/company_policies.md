# Company Policies

## Work From Home
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.
Core collaboration hours are 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM local time.

## Leave Policy
Annual leave allowance is 24 paid days per calendar year.
Sick leave does not reduce annual leave balance and requires manager notification.

## Reimbursement
Expenses under $50 can be self-approved with receipt upload.
Expenses above $50 need pre-approval from the reporting manager.
Claims should be submitted within 30 days of purchase.
