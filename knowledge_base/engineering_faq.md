# Engineering FAQ

## Deployment Window
Production deployments are allowed Tuesday and Thursday between 2:00 PM and 5:00 PM UTC.
Emergency fixes can be deployed at any time with incident channel approval.

## Code Review Rule
Every pull request requires at least one reviewer approval and all CI checks passing.
Security-sensitive changes require two approvals.

## Incident Priority
P1: Full outage or severe data corruption. Response target: 15 minutes.
P2: Major functionality degraded. Response target: 1 hour.
P3: Minor issue with workaround. Response target: 1 business day.
